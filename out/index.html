The Departure of Vell

The Departure of Vell is a 2-part work: it includes a Tarot Deck designed by Maggie Sullivan and a collaborative storytelling game written by Autumn Shelby. The game uses a Tarot Deck to represent and select aspects of the story being told.

Room in Delles

intaglio on paper

night at the pond

the moon is slim. a train rolls by. a gentle beast whose yellow lights from there within now phase between the silhouettes of shadow trees across the pond. the gentle wind in silences abides this hush. as circle stones all overgrown left standing lush — they croon to me.
